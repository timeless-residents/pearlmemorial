# Partner with Pearl Memorial
## Building Community Resilience Together

---

### Why This Matters to Your Business

The 2023 Lahaina fires showed us: **when communities suffer, everyone suffers.** Hotels lose staff. Restaurants lose customers. Gas stations lose power. Churches lose congregations.

Pearl Memorial works directly with Maui communities to build **off-grid resilience systems** — power, communication, and emergency preparedness that keeps families and businesses running when disaster strikes.
