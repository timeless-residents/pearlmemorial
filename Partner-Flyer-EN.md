### Why This Matters to Your Business

The 2023 Lahaina fires showed us: **when communities suffer, tourism suffers.** Your employees, your guests' experiences, and your operations all depend on resilient local infrastructure.

Pearl Memorial works directly with Maui communities to build **off-grid resilience systems** — power, communication, and emergency preparedness that keeps families safe when disaster strikes.
