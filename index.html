Pearl's Story

Named in Memory of Pearl (2007‚Äì2025)

For 18 years, Pearl‚Äîa toy poodle‚Äîlived as part of our family. He was there for everything, sharing our joys and sorrows.

In his final years, Pearl's body could no longer move freely. He needed care, and family outings became difficult. Visiting our hometown, traveling overnight, going far away‚Äîwe couldn't leave Pearl behind, but we couldn't take him either. For a long time, we gave up our freedom to travel.

In January 2025, Pearl quietly passed away.

Yet we couldn't let Pearl go. We placed his remains in a portable memorial made of shells and resin. In that moment, we realized: We could go anywhere with Pearl.

The homecoming trips we couldn't take while he was alive. Overseas travel. Showing Pearl the world. By creating a portable grave, we reclaimed our freedom to travel. And Pearl would travel to more places than he ever did in life.

This WiFi network carries his name. Pearl Memorial WiFi.

Our Roots in Maui

Ancestors' graves rest on this land

Our coming to Maui was no coincidence. Our main family line's ancestors sleep on this island. We bear the responsibility of grave keepers. We are here not as supporters, but as those rooted in this land.

01 Lahaina Strong

After the devastating 2023 Maui wildfires, we witnessed physical memories vanish in an instant‚Äîphotos, homes, keepsakes, gravestones. Memories are fragile. That's why we need to know how to protect them.

02 Global Perspective

In Maui, we visited Western and Eastern cemeteries alike. Weathered gravestones. Unreadable names. The same thing was happening everywhere. This is a challenge shared by all humanity.

Memorial Stone QR

Physical gravestones will eventually decay. Weathering, acid rain, earthquakes, typhoons, wildfires. No stone can defeat time and disaster.

But a QR code engraved on a gravestone connects to the digital world. When visitors scan it with their smartphones, the deceased's photos, videos, voice, and life records appear. Even if the gravestone crumbles, the memory remains.
