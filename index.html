Why We Engrave

From a birthtime unknown

Founder Takuya grew up not knowing the "beginning" of his own story. His birth time was unclear. No accurate record existed.

It may seem trivial -- just a birth time. But it's the first "imprint" of a life. Without it, you cannot cast a proper horoscope. You cannot ask the stars "Who am I?"

When your existence isn't engraved, you're left wondering: "Am I allowed to be here?"

That's why we engrave. To tell someone with certainty: "You were here. You existed. Your life had meaning."

Pearl's Story

Named in Memory of Pearl

2007 ‚Äì 2025

For 18 years, Pearl -- a toy poodle -- lived as part of our family. In his final years, his body couldn't move freely. Family outings became impossible. We gave up our freedom to travel.

In January 2025, Pearl quietly passed away.

We couldn't let him go. We placed his remains in a portable memorial made of shells and resin. In that moment, we realized:

We could go anywhere with Pearl. He would see the ocean he never saw in life.

By creating a portable grave, we reclaimed our freedom. And Pearl now travels to more places than he ever did in life.

This project carries his name. Pearl Memorial.
