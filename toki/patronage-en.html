Why patronage

Not an investment. Not a donation. A third way to participate.

We have seen memories disappear.

Through SoulCarrier's work surveying unclaimed graves, we read hundreds of headstones. Names weathered away. Lives erased. When we delivered remains to families, we were told: "No one remembers her anymore." Memories vanish if left alone. TokiStorage was born to change that reality.

Proof of existence must not remain a privilege.

The technology to preserve memories in quartz glass for a thousand years is ready. But we cannot let it remain a service only for the wealthy. The unclaimed dead, communities that lost their records in disasters, people who pass without a voice — everyone deserves to leave proof they existed.

Supporting the mission becomes your own proof of existence.

Patronage is neither investment nor charity. Your name and intention are inscribed in the millennium archive as part of this mission. When the activity you supported endures for a thousand years, that itself becomes your proof of existence.

Advancing R&D to make quartz glass inscription accessible to more people. Reducing cost and broadening access to proof of existence.
