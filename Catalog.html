Choosing Materials

Engraving Process

  1. Wash and dry material (24+ hours)
  2. Flatten surface (light sanding if needed)
  3. Create design (Canva, Illustrator, etc.)
  4. Test engrave (adjust power/speed on scrap)
  5. Final engrave
  6. Finish (oil for wood, clear coat for metal)
