## How To â€” Laser Engraving Basics

### Choosing Materials

| Material | Source | Characteristics | Difficulty |
|---|---|---|---|
| Palm leaves | Beach, garden | Returns to earth, lightweight | Easy |
| Driftwood | Beach | Natural shapes, one-of-a-kind | Easy |
| Coconut shell | Market, grocery | Hard, long-lasting | Medium |
| Lava rock | Hawaii local, garden stores | Heavy, lasts generations | Medium |
| Charcoal | BBQ supply stores | Black base, white engraving pops | Easy |
| Metal plate | Amazon, hardware stores | Permanent, outdoor-safe | Hard |

### Engraving Process

1. **Wash and dry material** (24+ hours)
2. **Flatten surface** (light sanding if needed)
3. **Create design** (Canva, Illustrator, etc.)
4. **Test engrave** (adjust power/speed on scrap)
5. **Final engrave**
6. **Finish** (oil for wood, clear coat for metal)

### Recommended Settings (LaserPecker 2 Pro)

| Material | Power | Speed | Passes |
|---|---|---|---|
| Wood | 80% | 60% | 1 |
| Palm leaf | 40% | 80% | 1 |
| Coconut shell | 100% | 40% | 2 |
| Charcoal | 30% | 90% | 1 |
